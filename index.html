The Problem

When a vehicle breaks down on the road, finding help is often stressful and time-consuming. Drivers face delays, safety concerns, and frustration trying to get reliable roadside assistance.

Our Solution

Our app provides a simple and effective way to connect drivers with nearby mechanics or electricians within a 30-yard radius. Using location tracking, we ensure help is on the way quickly and efficiently.

Key Features

  • Real-time location tracking and updates
  • 24/7 access to verified problem solvers
  • Secure in-app payments and cost estimates
  • Self-help guides and video tutorials for simple fixes
  • Emergency SOS alerts for enhanced safety
